What is CalgaryHacks?

CalgaryHacks is the premier student run Hackathon in Alberta! Since 2017 the University of Calgary Computer Science Undergraduate Society (CSUS) has challenged students with a 24-hour sprint programming competition to develop innovative solutions to a wide variety of problems. Groups of up-to 5 students (highschool to recent grads) have the opportunity to test their programming skills and network with our panel of tech professional judges.
